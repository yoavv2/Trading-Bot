`reconciliation.py`) with a pure, indexed, O(n) matcher that consumes the typed snapshots
from 09-01 and emits only closed-enum `Finding` values. Positions are resolved through a
single keyed map on `(symbol, account, side)`; orders/fills through their existing id maps.
