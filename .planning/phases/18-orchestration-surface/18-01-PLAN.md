<interfaces>
Existing persistence contracts to preserve:
- `Job.id` is a UUID primary key; Jobs and their audit history are never deleted by cancellation.
- `TimestampedModel` supplies `created_at` and `updated_at`.
- Migration 0018 is the current head and owns the `jobs` table.
- ORM models are exported from `trading_platform.db.models.__init__` and included in `__all__`.

New irreversible schema contract selected under Claude's discretion:
- Table/model: `job_mutations` / `JobMutation`.
- Columns: UUID `id`; `endpoint_id` String(128); `idempotency_key` String(255); `request_fingerprint` String(64); non-null UUID `job_id`; timestamps.
- Named unique constraint: `uq_job_mutations_endpoint_key` over (`endpoint_id`, `idempotency_key`).
- Named index: `ix_job_mutations_job_id`.
- `job_id` references `jobs.id` with `ondelete="RESTRICT"` so an idempotency record cannot outlive or silently lose its original Job.
</interfaces>
