Confirm rows span multiple run types (e.g. backtest, risk_evaluation, paper_execution) with status, session date, a "Started" timestamp, and an error indicator on any failed run.    a. SIGNALS & RISK (RUNS-03/04): on a run with risk activity, confirm the Signals sub-section shows signal_direction / signal_reason, and the Risk Decisions sub-section shows outcome / decision_code / decision_reason.    b. ORDERS & FILLS (RUNS-05): on a paper run, confirm orders show client_order_id (monospace) and, where present, the "supersedes: {client_order_id}" lineage line, and that fills render (symbol/side/quantity/price/filled_at).

Restart the API, Refresh, and confirm full recovery.
